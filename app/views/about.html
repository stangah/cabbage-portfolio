About Me

Grew up in both Taiwan and Minnesota. Exposed to both cultures and languages while being immersed in both palm trees and snow covered forests.

Went to a fine arts school in my hometown: Tainan, Taiwan. This is where I learned the foundation to visual arts.

I came to San Francisco the summer of 2012 to study Graphic Design at California College of the Arts.

I spent the summer of 2014 in Seattle, as a UX Design intern at Google.
